About

My name is Alex Hertz but feel free to call me Al. I design and develop the part of the internet you see and interact with. I'm always trying to find new ways to make the internet a more exciting place for creatives.

My specialties lie in user experience and interface design but I do quite a bit of front-end development as well. I strive to keep my work simple and intuitive and address every project from the standpoint that good design solves problems.

Over the past six or so years I have primarily worked as a freelance designer but have cut my teeth into advertising, and application design as well. Today, I work in the music industry helping artists connect with their listeners better through design and code.
